About

A bit about me

Hi, I'm Dileep. A self-taught product designer based in Bangalore. I focus on taking messy, early-stage ideas and turning them into clear, usable products.

I like working on things where the problem isn't fully defined yet. Where there's no playbook and you have to figure it out. I start by understanding what actually matters, then design something that makes sense for real people using it.

Recently, I've been designing and building products with AI assistants. I go from idea to a shipped, working product much faster now. I can design it, prototype it, and actually build it. That's been a real advantage, especially when working with startups that need to move fast.

I care about making products feel simple without dumbing them down. Good design to me is when something just works, scales well, and solves the right problem.
